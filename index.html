Exploratory Data Analysis of Wine Quality in R

In this project, I applied exploratory data analysis techniques to examine distributions, outliers and relationships between variables in a data set containing physicochemical properties of red and white wines. I identified the parameters that influence wine quality the most and created a simple regression model to predict wine density based on the concentrations of its ingredients.
